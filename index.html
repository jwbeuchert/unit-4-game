How to play the game


  1. There is a ramdom number that appears once the game starts."Number to Get"
  2. There are four Pirate Flags below for you to click on.
    • Moody
    • Teach
    • Rackham
    • Bonnet
  3. There are 4 pirate flags below. Each flag will represent a random number between 1 - 12. By cliking on a flag, your score will increase.
  4. To win the game, you will need to match your score to the random number above. If your score goes above the random numbers score, you lose.
  5. The values of the pirate flags will be hidden to you until you click on them.
  6. Whenever the game starts, there will be a new number generated. Also the numbers for the pirate flags will also change every round.
